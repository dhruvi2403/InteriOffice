Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a variety of spaces to meet your needs:

  • Conference rooms (small, medium, and large)
  • Private offices
  • Event spaces
  • Coworking desks
  • Photography studios
  • Recording studios

Each space comes fully equipped with high-speed internet, comfortable furniture, and climate control.

Booking a space is simple:

  1. Create an account or log in to your existing account
  2. Browse available spaces and select one that meets your needs
  3. Choose your desired date and time
  4. Review space details and amenities
  5. Complete your booking with payment

You'll receive an immediate confirmation email with all the details of your booking, including access instructions and contact information.

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent:

  • 7+ days before booking: Full refund
  • 3-6 days before booking: 75% refund
  • 24-72 hours before booking: 50% refund
  • Less than 24 hours before booking: No refund

For rescheduling, we offer one free change with at least 48 hours' notice. Additional changes or changes with less notice may incur a fee of 15% of the booking value.

Special events and large bookings may have custom cancellation terms specified at the time of booking.

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. Your booking includes:

  • Space rental for the duration specified
  • Basic utilities (electricity, water, heating/cooling)
  • Standard WiFi connection
  • Basic cleaning services

Additional costs that may apply (always clearly displayed before checkout):

  • Premium WiFi upgrade for large groups
  • Equipment rentals
  • After-hours access fee
  • Excessive cleaning fee (if applicable)
  • Damage deposit (refundable)

All applicable taxes will be shown during the checkout process.
