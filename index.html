Frequently Asked Questions

What types of spaces can I book?

We offer a variety of spaces to meet your needs:

  • Conference rooms (small, medium, and large)
  • Private offices
  • Event spaces
  • Coworking desks
How do I book a space?

Booking a space is simple:

  1. Create an account or log in to your existing account
  2. Browse available spaces and select one that meets your needs
  3. fill out the contact us form and our team will reach to you as soon as possible
Can I customize a space for my space?

Yes! We offer several customization options for your space:

  • Flexible seating arrangements
  • Additional partitions
  • Differnt sized rooms based on space available
What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent:

  • 30+ days before start of work: Full refund
  • 15 days before booking: 75% refund
  • 5 days before booking: no refund
Are there any hidden fees?

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. Your booking cost covers all the things including the electricity required for construction. However if any extra changes are required from either of the party then it might cost extra.
